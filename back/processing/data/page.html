The development working group

The development working group is a business expert group formed of Datahub's customers to support the development of the Datahub system. The working group gives recommendations on the business appropriateness and the suitability of development proposals for the Datahub system.

A subgroup consisting of system vendors operates under the development working group.

More frequent delivery of metering data

The working group for more frequent delivery of metering data focuses on producing a business model for this initiative. In collaboration with the Datahub system vendor, the group is also defining the data interface for the updated metering data delivery process, allowing the industry sufficient time to adapt to the new interface.

The premise for the working group for more frequent delivery of metering data is the government decree 767/2021 “Valtioneuvoston asetus sähköntoimitusten selvityksestä ja mittauksesta” chapter 6, section 5, which states that:

  • “The information system handling the DSOs’ metering data must collect registered metering data from new remote meters to the meter reading system at least every six hours.”
